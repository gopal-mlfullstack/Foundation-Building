Adding Animations to Your Site

Published on Oct 26, 2025 • 6 min read

Animations add life to your website. They make interactions smoother and guide the user’s focus to important parts of the page.

You can use simple CSS transitions for buttons, cards, and hover effects — or go advanced with keyframes for sliding and fading elements.

Example:


button:hover {
  transform: scale(1.05);
  transition: transform 0.3s ease;
}

Always use animations subtly — they should enhance, not distract.
