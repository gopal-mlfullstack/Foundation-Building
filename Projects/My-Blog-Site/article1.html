Building My First Website

Published on Oct 24, 2025 • 5 min read

Building my first website was a huge milestone. I started with just HTML and CSS, learning how to structure a page, add colors, and adjust spacing using margin and padding.

The most important lesson I learned was: keep it simple. Focus on clean layout, readable fonts, and consistent colors. Once that’s done, you can add more advanced features like responsive design and animations.

Over time, I realized that front-end web development is a mix of art and logic. With every new project, your confidence grows — so start today!
